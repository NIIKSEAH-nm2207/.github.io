about this website

I made this website to analyse the data of the top 250 anime of 2023.

Using this data, I hope to analyse viewers' preferences: be it in terms of type of anime watched (TV, movie, etc.), animation studio (which tells us about art style preference), duration of episodes, or even genre of anime.

With this analysis, producers are able to better understand the needs and wants of their audience and make content catered to them.
